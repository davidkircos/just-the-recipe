### Ingredients

- 1lb ground Lamb meat
- 1 Habanero pepper
- Garlic 3 cloves
- Pita bread
- Tzatziki sauce

### Instructions

1. Add ground lamb to pan
2. Add garlic and habanero pepper
3. Cook until crumbled and brown
4. In a second pan, fry pita with olive oil
5. Serve lamb on pita with Tzatziki
